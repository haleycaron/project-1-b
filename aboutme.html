About Me

Biography

Haley Caron is a indie-pop/rock singer-songwriter from Portland, Maine. Her main influences for both musical and lyrical style include Phoebe Bridgers, girl in red, Lana Del Rey, and Freddie Mercury. She traveled over 1,600 miles to pursue her dreams at Loyola University New Orleans. She is currently majoring in Music Industry Studies with a concentration in business. Haley enjoys learning about all aspects of the music industry with effort to excel in her future career. She has had a passion for music ever since she was a little girl. She participated in musicals and choir during elementary, middle, and high school. She is now in the midst of multiple projects, including her highly anticipated first single, expected to be released within the next few months. As music has always been her refuge, she seeks to create raw, cutting music that tells her story while simultaneously connecting to her audience.
